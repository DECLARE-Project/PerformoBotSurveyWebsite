Thank you for participating in our study and helping us to evaluate PerformoBot! The study consists of two parts and should take around 20 minutes.

As soon as you press the Start button below, you will be presented with a task in which you have to evaluate the performance of an exemplary software system with the help of PerformoBot.
You have up to seven minutes to complete the task, which should be enough time to complete it without a problem. A timer on the left will show you how much time you have left.
The interaction history with PerformoBot will be saved to evaluate the chatbot.

After you have completed the task or the time is up we will present you a link to a survey. In the survey we ask you to give us some information about previous experiences with chatbots or performance evaluations that you might had before participating in the study.
Furthermore, we will ask you several questions about your interaction with PerformoBot during the task in order to evaluate the chatbot and improve it in the future.
If you want, you can interact with PerformoBot on the start page while you complete the survey.

Please complete the survey directly after you completed the task because otherwise it will be difficult for us to connect your interaction with the chatbot to your answers in the survey.

Are you ready to start working on the performance evaluation task? Then press the Start button to begin!
